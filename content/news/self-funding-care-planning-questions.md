Self-funding families often have to make fast decisions while trying to protect safety, dignity and long-term options.

## Questions to ask early

- What care needs are stable, and what is changing?
- What risks are present overnight, during transfers or around medication?
- What support does the family realistically have capacity to provide?
- How will the care package be reviewed?
- Who will document changes and incidents?
- When should financial, legal or clinical advice be brought in?

Care planning is not only about arranging support for today. It is about reducing avoidable risk as needs change.
